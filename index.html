Tic Tac Toe Tactic

Imagine Tic Tac Toe where the first player move doesn't always have the advantage. Imagine Tic Tac Toe where the fun doesn't nearly have to end so quickly. Now imagine Tic Tac Toe on 6 different planes like the side of a Rubik's Cube. This is the concept behind the new Tic Tac Toe Tactic! Tic Tac Toe Tactic brings the traditional classic game of Tic Tac Toe into the the next level by adding dimensions of gameplay boards(planes) as playing fields.
